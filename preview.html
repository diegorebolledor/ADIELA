A Legacy Written in Stone

For over 500 years, Colombian emeralds have been treasured as symbols of protection, prosperity, and prestige. Our heritage collection honors this ancient tradition.

Ancient Traditions

Before the Spanish conquest, the Muisca people considered emeralds sacred stones of protection and divine connection. These precious gems were offerings to their gods, symbols of fertility and renewal.

Today, we honor this rich heritage by crafting each piece with the same reverence and attention to detail that has been passed down through generations.
